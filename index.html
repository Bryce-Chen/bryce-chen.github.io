About Me

I am a second-year PhD student at the University of Washington, advised by Prof. Mari Ostendorf. My current research interests focus on building multimodal models to address real-world problems involving speech, text, and image modalities. Prior to this, I graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a B.S. in Computer Engineering and Computer Science, where I had the privilege of working with Prof. Kangwook Lee.
